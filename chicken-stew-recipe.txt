Chicken Stew

What You Need
1-1/2 lb.  boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/4 cup  flour
1 Tb salt
1 pkg.  (8 oz.) sliced fresh mushrooms
2 cups  baby carrots
1 small onion, chopped
1 can  (14-1/2 oz.) fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth
1 cup  frozen peas, thawed, drained
1/2 cup  (1/2 of 8-oz. tub) PHILADELPHIA Chive & Onion Cream Cheese Spread

Add splash Worcestershire - good - try 1 Tb

Also - add 1 Tb salt to chicken

Make It
TOSS chicken with flour & salt in slow cooker. 
ADD all remaining ingredients except peas and cream cheese spread; cover with lid. 
COOK on LOW 6 to 8 hours (or on HIGH 3 to 4 hours). Stir in cream cheese and peas; cook, covered, 30 min.
